# QUORUM SENSING · QRM

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LIFE SCIENCE**.

QUORUM SENSING (QRM) — a mind made of many: bacteria count themselves — each leaks a signaling molecule (an autoinducer) and senses how much is around — and once density crosses a threshold the whole population switches on at once: glowing, building a biofilm, attacking. First understood in Vibrio fischeri, the bacterium that lights the bobtail squid; Bonnie Bassler showed an inter-species dialect (AI-2). Decision with no decider; blocking the signal ('quorum quenching') is a real antibacterial strategy. LIVE interactive: an illustrative density switch (add cells past the quorum and they glow in unison). Two-layer honest; living researchers (Bassler/Greenberg/Nealson) cited not minted. 10 emergents.
